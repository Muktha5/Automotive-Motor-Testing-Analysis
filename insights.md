# 🧠 Key Insights from Analysis

## 🔹 Performance Behavior

* Motor performance differs based on direction due to load variation
* Increased resistance leads to higher energy consumption

## 🔹 Durability Trends

* Repeated cycles introduce gradual changes in performance
* These changes indicate normal wear and system adaptation

## 🔹 Thermal Impact

* Temperature plays a major role in motor efficiency
* Stable thermal response indicates better design reliability

## 🔹 Stability vs Variation

* Consistent performance indicates robustness
* Fluctuations may indicate inefficiencies or early wear

## 🔹 Engineering Understanding

* Durability testing is essential for product validation
* Small variations can lead to long-term performance differences
